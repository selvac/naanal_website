About Us

Naanal’s goal is to make it as easy as possible to deploy and manage applications, platforms and infrastructure for any business needs. Naanal is pioneering a simplified, modular, service-based IT deployment process, where fully configured components of an application/platform/infrastructure architecture are encapsulated as “Stack” and made available as a service. As a result, Application, Platform, Infrastructure deployment, and management for the enterprise is now a seamless process.
